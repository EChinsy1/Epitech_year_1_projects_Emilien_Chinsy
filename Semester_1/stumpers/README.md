# Stumpers
**These are Stumpers**
Stumpers are two hour long exams where we can only use a tty or terminal and no internet.

Projects:

1. **sortwords**
    - Description: we got a sentence as an entry and had to print out the words sorted
    - [subject](./subjects/sortwords.pdf)
2. **anagram**
    - Description: takes two strings as a parameter and says if they are anagrams
    - [subject](./subjects/anagram.pdf)
3. **fozzbazz**
    - Description: takes two numbers and prints fozz or bazz depending on their multiples
    - [subject](./subjects/fozzbazz.pdf)
3. **geekNameFormatter**
    - Description: formats a sentence in a specific way
    - [subject](./subjects/geekNameFormatter.pdf)
